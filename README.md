# dynamic-pricing-strategy-
A data-driven dynamic pricing system that adjusts product prices in real time based on demand, competition, customer behavior, and market conditions to maximize revenue and profitability.

Overview:
This project implements a Dynamic Pricing Strategy using data analytics and machine learning techniques. The system analyzes various factors such as demand trends, customer behavior, seasonal patterns, inventory levels, and competitor pricing to recommend optimal product prices.
The goal is to maximize revenue, improve customer satisfaction, and maintain market competitiveness through intelligent price adjustments.

Features:
Real-time price optimization
Demand-based pricing analysis
Competitor price monitoring
Customer behavior analysis
Seasonal trend detection
Revenue and profit maximization
Data visualization dashboards
Predictive pricing recommendations

Technologies Used:
Python
Pandas
NumPy
Scikit-learn
Matplotlib / Seaborn
Jupyter Notebook

Workflow:
Collect and preprocess pricing data.
Analyze demand and market trends.
Train machine learning models for price prediction.
Generate optimal pricing recommendations.
Visualize pricing insights and business impact.

Business Impact:
The system helps businesses make data-driven pricing decisions, increase sales, improve profitability, and respond quickly to changing market conditions.
